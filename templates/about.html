About

Welcome to The Tasting Experience. If you want to start developing a cultural taste and increase your knowlegde about several drinks, all with their own interesting characteristics, you are at the right place! Why do you want to develop a taste of your "own"? One reason could be for social drinking. Social drinking, also commonly referred to as responsible drinking, refers to casual drinking of alcoholic beverages in a social setting without an intent to become intoxicated. In Western cultures, good news is often celebrated by a group of people having a few alcoholic drinks. For example, alcoholic drinks may be served to "wet the baby's head" in the celebration of a birth. Buying someone an alcoholic drink is often considered a gesture of goodwill. It may be an expression of gratitude, or it may mark the resolution of a dispute.
